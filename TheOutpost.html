Game Projects

The Outpost

Overview

The Outpost prototype is a FPS Action-Adventure game with survival mechanics. This game puts the you in the role of a space mercenary that has been dispatched to a hostile planet to investigate why there has been contact with the research station on the planet.

The player manage health, thirst, stamina, and battery-power to survive. As the player investigates what happened to the outpost, the player must defend against whatever has awoken on the planet.

Role: Lead Technical Designer

Team size: 1 - 2

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Main Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Created player system and gameplay mechanic
  • Developed Enemy AI System
  • Designed prototype levels 1, 2, and 3
  • Developed Spider Environmental Actors to aid in Enviromental Storytelling
  • Planned and delegated projects tasks to keep project in scope and on schedule

Prototype Actionblocking

Once the systems and gameplay mechanics have been devised, the next step is to test these systems and gameplay mechanics in an action block prototype. In this action block prototype, both system mechanics and gameplay mechanics were prototyped and tested before moving on to the first iteration. The following systems and gameplay mechanics were devised in this action block:

  • System mechanics for health, thirst, stamina, and battery power
  • Point System fo determining player damage ability
  • Flashlight ability to allow player to see in a darkened area
  • Battery power mechanic linking both the player ammo and flashlight ability
  • Enemy AI that tracks player movement and does damage upon impact
  • Enemy spawning object that spawns enemies into the environment

1st Pass Prototype

The first iteration of the Outpost prototype puts the system and gameplay mechanics into context by providing an introductory level to test out the mechanics in gameplay. Here, a basic level is provided to allow the player to taste the gameplay that lies ahead. The following was initiated in this 1st Pass Prototype:

  • Crashed-landed ship to test flashlight ability in context
  • Hostile alien environment to provide environmental storytelling to the player
  • Multiple pick-up items for battery power, health, thirst, and stamina to provide player with ample opportunities to test game systems
  • Multiple enemy spawning objects to allow player the opportunity to experiece core gameplay
  • Large Nav Mesh Volume to test Enemy AI ability to track player in the level

2nd Pass Prototype

The second iteration of the Outpost prototype goes further of testing the systems and mechanics in a new environment and in an narrower environment. This second prototype brings ina new enemy AI that is paralyzed by the flashlight ability. This prototype begins to also delve deeper into the environmental storytelling component of the prototype. The following was initiated in this 2nd Pass Prototype:

  • New enemy AI that is paralyzed by the flashlight ability
  • A new environment that tests the systems and game mechanics in a narrower environment

3rd Pass Prototype

The final pass of the prototype brings all of the components together. This final prototype fully conceptualizes the systems and game mechanics, and provides the environmental storytelling aspect to entice the player to delve deeper into the outpost and find out what happened. The following was initiated in this 3nd Pass Prototype:

  • Added the force shield push ability for the player to push back enemies
  • Added new spider enemy AI that explode on contact
  • Added new spider environmental actors that add to the environmental storytelling
  • Added new weapons that the player can switch through
  • Created final level that brings all of the narrative components together

Enemy AI

The Enemy AI is designed to function on a Nav Mesh volume in the prototype.
The Enemy AI is programmed to sense and track the player's movement.
Once the player is in range, the AI will run torwards the player, and upon contact cause damage and explode.
