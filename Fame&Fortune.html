Fame & Fortune

Fame & Fortune is an Action-RPG that has four modes of gameplay to allow players both a rich single-player and multiplayer experience. In this game, the player chooses a core class of either a Barbarian, Knight, Magician, or Adventurer. Then the player must take on Oberon's Challenge the Ruler of the Outlands. As the player progresses the game, players will be able to increase their fame and fortune to be able to join either the Hall of Heroes, or the Dungeoneer's League, or even both. Numerous adventures, relics, and untold legends await players in the game Fame & Fortune.
