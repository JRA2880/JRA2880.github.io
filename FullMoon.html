Game Design Portfolio

Full Moon

Full Moon is a Third-Person Action-Adventure game that puts the player into the role of a victim of the curse of the werewolf. The starting point of this game design is the target audience member that seeks an action-packed game with opportunities for visual ques and fast reaction times. The core gameplay allows the player to take on the persona of a werewolf that must find a way to end the curse before the next full moon, or be trapped as a werewolf forever.
